About Us

By offering a completely integrated solution with real-time tracking, nutritional management, and individualized suggestions, our To-Be rich picture approach seeks to overcome the drawbacks of existing solutions. The existing solution has a very organized data management system where the data are maintained in different ways but in our proposed system we are trying to solve this problem by collecting data of the users through a single system that maintains the whole data process. To do this, our system makes use of MySQL databases for the centralized storing of patient demographics, diagnosis data, physician details, hospital information, and other vital data, guaranteeing data confidentiality, integrity, and convenient access for authorized users. Our system solves the problem of scheduling and taking appointments through the reception it has become fully automated which gives a perfect end-to-end system to book appointments with the physician as per their schedule and the time given by the physicians will be noted to acknowledge the effort of the physicians.
